What is Zenmaticai Salon Agent?

Zenmaticai Salon Agent is a powerful chatbot widget designed to be integrated directly into your salon's website. It acts as a 24/7 virtual receptionist, allowing your clients to book and manage appointments effortlessly through a conversational interface.

Purpose: Automating Your Appointment Scheduling

Our core purpose is to automate and streamline the appointment scheduling process for salons. By handling client requests, checking availability, and confirming bookings in real-time, Zenmaticai Salon Agent reduces administrative workload, minimizes booking errors, and ensures a smooth experience for both your staff and your clients.

Our Use of Google Calendar Data

To provide this service, Zenmaticai Salon Agent connects with your Google Calendar. This connection is essential for the application to function correctly.

This application connects to your Google Calendar to **view and create new appointments on your behalf**. We will only access the calendars you explicitly authorize during the setup process. We do not access any other data in your Google Account. All data accessed is used exclusively for the purpose of scheduling and managing salon appointments.
